Hello world, I am Lukas!

I'm a Software Engineer with a deep-rooted passion for crafting elegant and effective software solutions. My expertise lies primarily in building desktop and web applications using the C#/.NET stack. I'm committed to writing clean, maintainable code and adhering to sound software engineering principles.

Beyond my core skills, I have a keen interest in exploring the frontiers of software development. Distributed Systems, Domain Driven Design, Security, and AI are areas that captivate my attention and drive my professional growth. I believe in sharing knowledge and actively contribute to the developer community through talks and workshops at conferences.

I'm excited about the opportunity to connect with others who share my enthusiasm for building exceptional software.
